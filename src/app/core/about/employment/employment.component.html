I spent 9 years and majority of my development career working at the wonderful Lixar I.T.
Over the course of my 9 year career, I had the pleasure of developing iOS apps in the Auto, Flight and Government spaces.

I was able to demonstrate my skills and leadership to progress between a Junior Mobile Developer to Intermediate Mobile Developer to finally a Senior Mobile Developer.
While I primarily focused on iOS development, I had plenty of opportunity to work in web and android spaces as well as backend services.

Lixar gave me the opportunity to learn project management through tools such as JIRA, Azure and Amazon Web Services. I was also exposed and eventually gained the knowledge to use and maintain CI/CD services such as Bamboo, Teamcity, Azure Pipelines and Amazon Pipelines.

Some of the projects I worked on included an iPhone app that communicated wirelessly with an automobile through a piece of hardware inserted in the OBD port, an internal iPad app called Flydesk that provided services for pilots flying for Allegiant Airlines, the Allegiant Airlines iOS consumer app, and a yet to be released iOS app for Government of Canada CBSA employees to inspect and review packages electronically. Please take a look at the 'PROJECTS' tab to take a look at everything I was a part of and the detailed list of technologies used by all of them.
